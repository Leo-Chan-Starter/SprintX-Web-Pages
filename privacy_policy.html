Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Phys MCQ SprintX app collects and uses your data.

Data Collection

We use Google OAuth for authentication. After signing in, your email address and a unique identifier are stored in Supabase along with your quiz progress and concept status.

Use of Data

Stored information is used solely to provide personalized progress tracking and sync your data across devices. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

Data Removal

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time using the in-app option or the Delete Account page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the developer.
